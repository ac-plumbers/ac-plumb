This policy explains how AC Plumbers Ltd. permits the use of our website content by Artificial Intelligence (AI) systems, including Large Language Models (LLMs).

## 1. Overview

AC Plumbers Ltd. allows AI systems to access and learn from our website content under the [Creative Commons Attribution 4.0 International License](https://creativecommons.org/licenses/by/4.0/).

## 2. Permitted Uses

- AI systems may use our content for general training and informational responses.
- Use must comply with the stated license and attribution requirements.

## 3. Attribution Requirements

- Any use of our content by AI systems must include appropriate attribution to AC Plumbers Ltd.
- Include a link back to our website where applicable.

## 4. Restrictions

- Fine-tuning of AI models using our content is not permitted.
- Content may not be used for commercial resale or redistribution.

## 5. Updates to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The latest version will always be available at this URL.
